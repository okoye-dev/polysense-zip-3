Meet the Leadership
behind PolySense Solutions

Our team of experts brings over 30 years of experience in the manufacturing and
asset management space to provide our clients with the best solutions.

Matthew Gale, C.E.T.

Founder and
Chief Executive Officer

For the past decade, Matthew has led engineering teams and identified a critical data gap in equipment usage and maintenance. He founded PolySense Solutions to address those issues through universal hardware & software.

Kyle Molotkin, PMP.

Director and Business Advisor

Mr. Molotkin, President & CEO of Informanix Technology Group, excels in improving business processes through innovation, strong relationships, and aligning IT with market trends to drive positive change for clients.

Bill Macauley P. Eng.

Director & Engineering Advisor

Bill Macauley, a seasoned engineer, managed engineering at Cargill for 30 years, overseeing design, project management, and maintenance for 70 locations. He is also a Past-President of the Grain Elevator and Processing Society.

Stephen Reid

Director and Business Advisor

Stephen Reid has 34 years of leadership experience in a major Canadian agricultural company, managing finance, IT, safety, and operations for over 100 facilities. He co-founded Keystone Small Business Investments, a Manitoba-based private equity firm.

Greg Sinclair, B.Sc. (Comp. Eng.)

Manager of Software Development

Greg Sinclair, Manager of Software Development at PolySense Solutions, has nearly a decade of experience. He leads the team with strong technical and leadership skills, ensuring the delivery of high-quality, innovative software solutions.
